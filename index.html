About Daniel

Hi there, my name's Daniel and I'm a full-stack web developer in Seattle. In a previous life, I was a collegiately-trained musician, where I solved both technical and artistic problems through innovation, creativity, collaboration, and persistent focus. By leveraging those strengths into my passion for technology and design, I'm able to create elegant applications with both substance and flash.

I have experience in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Sequelize, Bcrypt, Git/Github, Heroku, AJAX, and JSON.
